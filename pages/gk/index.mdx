## General Knowledge

"GK" is an abbreviation commonly used to refer to General Knowledge. General Knowledge is a broad term that encompasses a wide range of information about various subjects including history, geography, science, current affairs, sports, arts, literature, and more. It represents knowledge that is not specific to a particular academic or professional field but is considered valuable for individuals to have a well-rounded understanding of the world. General Knowledge is often tested in educational settings, competitive exams, and trivia quizzes, and is considered important for staying informed and having meaningful conversations.
